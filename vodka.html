Vodka Cocktails

Pornstar Martini

Probably my favorate cocktail bonus points that it comes with a shot of Prosseco!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe passion fruits, halved
  • 60ml vanilla vodka
  • 30ml passoa
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp sugar syrup
  • prosecco , to serve

How To make

  1. Put the Vodka,passoa,lime juice and sugar syrup into a cocktail shaker with ice and shake well
  2. Pour into a martini glass and add a halfed passoin fruit
  3. Pour a shot of Prosseco to go along side your drink

French Martini

Had this when I went to a spa weekend with my husband as I wanted a change from Pornstar Martini, the risk was worth it!

Ingredients

  • 40ml vodka
  • 20ml Chambord liqueur
  • 60ml pressed pineapple juice
  • pineapple wedge, to serve
  1. Add the Vodka,Chambord and Pineapple juice to a cocktail shaker with plenty of ice
  2. shake well until blended
  3. Pour into a martini glass and serve with a pineapple wedge
