Rum Cocktails

Mojito

A classic, perfect for hot summer days

Ingredients

  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Limes
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • White Rum
  • Soda water

How To make

  1. Place the mint leaves and cut up lime into a glass and muddle to relese the juice
  2. Add the sugar to the mix and fill the glass with ice
  3. add the rum and top up with soda water
