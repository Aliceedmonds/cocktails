              <h2>Long Island Iced Tea</h2> 
              <p>This cocktail is sure to get you drunk fast as it has <em>Everything</em> in it!</p>
              <h3>Ingredients</h3>
              <ul>
                <li>Vodka</li>  
                <li>Gin</li>
                <li>Tequila</li>
                <li>Rum</li>
                <li>Lime Juice</li>
                <li>Cola</li>
                </ul>
            
          
              <ol>
                  <li>Mix vodka,gin,tequila,rum and lime juice in a cocktail shaker </li>

      <h2>Bramble</h2>
              <p>This is a cocktail thats perfect for summer evenings</p>
              <h3>Ingredients</h3>
              <ul>
                  <li>50ml gin</li>
                  <li>25ml lemon juice</li> 
                  <li>¾tbsp sugar syrup</li>
                  <li>ice cubes</li>
                  <li>crushed ice</li>
                  <li>¾tbsp crème de mure</li>
                  <li>1 lemon slice and a blackberry to serve</li>
              </ul>
              <ol>
                  <li>Mix the gin, lemon juice and sugar syrup in a cocktail shaker with the ice cubes</li>
                  <li>Add the cruched ice to a short tumbler glass and pour over what is in the cocktail shaker</li>
                  <li>Drizzle over the crème de mure and garnish with the lemon and blackberry</li>
              </ol>
